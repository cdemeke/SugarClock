What is it?

Your glucose, always visible

SugarClock turns the Ulanzi TC001 pixel clock into a dedicated glucose display. It shows your current reading in big, bright numbers you can see from across the room — no phone unlocking needed.

🎨

Color-coded readings

Green when you're in range, orange for highs and lows, red for urgent values. Know your status at a glance without reading the number.

↑

Trend arrows

See which direction your glucose is heading with clear arrows: rising fast, rising slowly, holding steady, falling slowly, or dropping fast.

🔔

Customizable alerts

Set a buzzer to sound when your glucose goes above or below your personal thresholds. Includes a snooze button so it won't keep beeping.

🌐

Works with Dexcom & more

Connects directly to Dexcom Share (US & International) or any custom glucose data source like Nightscout.

☀️

Auto brightness

Built-in light sensor adjusts brightness automatically. Bright enough to read in daylight, dim enough for your bedside at night.

📱

Web dashboard

Configure everything from any phone, tablet, or computer on your WiFi network. No apps to install — just open a browser.

How it works

Up and running in three steps

No coding experience needed. If you can plug in a USB cable and use a web browser, you can do this.

1

Get the hardware

Order a Ulanzi TC001 pixel clock online — it's around $40 and available on Amazon and other retailers. You'll also need a USB-C cable (one is usually included).

It looks like a regular desk clock. Nobody will know it's a glucose monitor unless you tell them!
📦 Photo: Ulanzi TC001 in its box
2

Flash the firmware

Connect the clock to your computer with the USB cable and use our step-by-step guide to install the SugarClock software. We walk you through every click.

Don't worry — "flashing" just means copying the software onto the clock. It's safe and reversible.
🔌 Photo: Clock connected to a laptop via USB-C cable
3

Connect your glucose data

Open the setup page on your phone or computer, enter your WiFi password and Dexcom login (or Nightscout URL), and you're done. Readings appear within a minute.

Everything is configured through a friendly web page — no command line or code editing required.
